---
title: "Proposals and approvals"
description: "How administrators review and approve changes to Mothy's skills and automations before they take effect."
---

Proposals are how anything that changes Mothy's behaviour gets reviewed before it
takes effect. Mothy can draft a change to a skill or an automation, but it is
always a person who decides. This page is for administrators.

## Reviewing a proposal

Pending proposals appear on the Proposals screen. Each one shows what would change,
and for an edit the old and new side by side, together with the findings of an
automatic safety check on the content. That check blocks anything containing
hidden or disguised text outright, and flags anything that looks like a credential
or an attempt to override Mothy's rules, so you see it before you decide.

## Approving or rejecting

Approving applies the change and records it, tied to the exact content approved, so
a later alteration would be detectable. Rejecting discards it. Two administrators
cannot both approve the same proposal by accident; it applies once. Every decision,
approve or reject, is on the audit trail.
