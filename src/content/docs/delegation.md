---
title: "Delegation"
description: "How Mothy splits a large task across restricted helpers that report back."
---

For a large request, such as checking a question across many documents or
cross-referencing several policies at once, Mothy can split the work across
helpers that run at the same time and return their findings.

Each helper starts fresh with only the task it was given, can only look things up
(it cannot change anything or start further helpers), and acts as you, with your
access, never more. Only the helpers' summaries come back into your conversation.
Every step each helper takes is recorded on the audit trail, so a delegated task
can be reconstructed in full later.
